Background theory

To built a electric machine and let it change the mechanical to the electric power we need to know some theory .Here are some important theory that participate.

[1]The theory of the Power generation principle

Before we realize the principle of wind turbine generator, let us see some background. If classifying the wind turbines from spindle direction, we can classify them into the horizontal spindle wind turbines and vertical spindle wind turbines. For wind power generator with horizontal spindle wind turbine, we usually use the control system to keep the wind spindle in the windward state, making sure that the rotor rotates successfully.

The principle of wind turbine generator is to convert wind energy into mechanical energy and then into electrical energy. The wind flows through the blades at a certain speed and angle, making the wind wheel obtain the moment, which means the force makes the object rotate around the rotation axis. The rotor (composed of blades and turbine) usually has a large quantity - a single blade of a large wind turbine weighs several tons, so considering its inertia, the design speed of the wind wheel is low, usually 20~30r/min. Then, the lower speed input by the wind turbine is increased to 1000~1500 r/min through the speed increase gearbox. In this way, with the increase of the rotation speed of the rotor inside the generator, its cutting speed of the magnetic field can meet the power generation needs of the generator. People always believe that the bigger power the generation makes, the better it is. However, this thought is a wrong, because the wind power generator is only a device used to charging the battery, and the power is determined by the battery. [2]The secret of the gear ratio

Gear transmission system is one of an important parts of wind turbine generator model. Under normal conditions, the wind turbine rotates very slowly,which far below the speed required by the generator for power generation. According to the analysis of physical theory, when two gears with different radii mesh and rotate, their linear speeds are the same, so the gear with smaller radius has larger angular speed and faster speed. Therefore, by using gear set transmission, the lower wind turbine speed can be changed into a higher generator speed, thus the wind turbine can generate electricity even in a small wind speed. When installing gears, there are three gear ratios: 2:1, 4:1 and 8:1 for us to choose. To verify the above analysis, we use different gear sets for transmission and measure the generator output voltage respectively. We found that when the gear ratio is 8:1, the generator output voltage is the maximum, and when the gear ratio is 2:1, the generator output voltage is the minimum.And when the gear ratio is 4:1, the output voltage of the generator is between the two. According to the above theory and experimental results, we choose 8:1 gear ratio for transmission. In addition, we once considered using three gears for transmission. However, the experiment found that it could not change the gear ratio, and would increase the friction consumption between gears and reduce the power generation efficiency of the generator, so it was not adopted in the end.Moreover,when installing the gear, the position of the generator should be flexibly adjusted according to the size of the gear to ensure that the two gears mesh normally and reduce friction loss.

Methodology - Equipment and procedure

When we were testing the power of the wind turbine, we took three variables into account, they were the shape of the blade, the distance to the fan, and the speed of the wind. Obviously they were affecting the result to some extent. Therefore, we controlled the variables to see which variable contributed most to the result and to find the best choice of each pair of variable. Our findings are as follow.

Firstly, we take the shape of the blade into consideration, since different shapes will contribute a lot to the result of the output of the power. We made the blades in the shape of streamline, because we thought most wings of airplanes were in this shape, which could make airplanes fly faster. However, the result was out of our expectations—the blades hardly spun, and the voltage and the current were very low, thus, the output of the power was merely 0.00015W. Therefore, we change our thoughts and made some blades in the shape of diamond. We believed the shape of diamond could make use of the wind to the most extent. By comparison, when the blades were in the shape of diamond, the output of power was 0.383W, much higher than the previous data. So in the next stages we used the blades in the shape of diamond.

Then we change the distance, we guess that when we reduce the distance ,the output of the wind turbine will be increasing, but in fact not ,we got the number of voltage is0.35V,and the current is 0.003A, not a good numerical value. after trying and trying we find that at a specific distance ,maybe 50cm ,the turbine goes the fastest.it turns out that the direction of the wind plays an important role, when the distance was too small, the wind gives out a vertical force to block the blade, so we need to find the best, through testing and testing ,we find the most suitab
